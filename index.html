David Klindt

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the group of Benjamin Dunn, where I work on machine learning, computational neuroscience and computer vision.

Prior to that, I did my PhD at the University of Tübingen, where I was advised by Matthias Bethge and Thomas Euler and worked on biological and computer vision. I did my masters in Brain and Mind Sciences at University College London, École Normale Supérieure and Pierre and Marie Curie University in Paris. I did my bachelors at the Otto von Guericke University.
